I lead the Embedding & Search team at <a href="https://www.twelvelabs.io">TwelveLabs</a>, where we build multimodal foundation models for video understanding.
I'm the first author of <a href="https://github.com/dandelin/ViLT">ViLT</a>, one of the early works that shaped efficient vision-language architectures.
Previously, I was a research scientist at <a href="https://naver-career.gitbook.io/en/teams/clova-cic">Naver AI LAB</a> and <a href="https://www.kakaocorp.com/?lang=en">Kakao</a>, and I hold an <a href="http://hcil.snu.ac.kr/people/wonjae-kim">M.Sc.</a> and <a href="https://cse.snu.ac.kr/en">B.Sc.</a> from Seoul National University.

My current research focuses on:

- Multimodal Representation Learning (video, audio, text)
- Large-scale Embedding & Search Systems
- User Behavior Modeling for Search
